0-5 years


In the first five years of life, the world is a place of discovery and wonder. From the moment of birth, I was surrounded by the love and care of family, who provided warmth, security, and nourishment. My earliest memories are filled with the soothing sounds of lullabies, the comfort of a parent's embrace, and the playful curiosity that defined my days. As I grew, I learned to crawl, walk, and talk, each milestone bringing a new sense of independence and exploration. The world was my playground, and everything was a source of fascination—from the vibrant colors of toys to the enchanting stories read aloud at bedtime. I experienced the joy of laughter, the frustration of falling, and the comforting reassurance of a hand helping me back up. These early years were marked by a series of firsts: my first steps, first words, and first encounters with the world around me. I began to form connections with other children, learning to share, play, and express my emotions. The simple pleasures of playing in the park, splashing in puddles, and building sandcastles were my greatest adventures. In these formative years, I developed a sense of self and a foundational understanding of the world, setting the stage for the journey ahead.
